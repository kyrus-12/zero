What is AIGHAM?

AIGHAM 7 is an advanced, next-generation artificial intelligence tutoring system designed to enhance science education. It provides personalized learning through multiple complementary pathways:

  • Descriptor Path: Offers comprehensive, media-rich lessons with animated visuals and detailed theoretical explanations to deepen conceptual understanding.
  • Skill Path: Features interactive, level-based quizzes (Level 1 to Level 4) with immediate feedback, promoting mastery and reinforcing cognitive skills.
  • Simulation and Laboratory Path: Provides virtual experiments and simple simulations that allow students to explore scientific concepts in a safe, interactive environment.
